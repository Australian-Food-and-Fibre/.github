# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment. All participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all communications
- Focus on what is best for the project and organisation
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Maintain confidentiality of proprietary information and credentials

## Scope

This applies to all Australian Food and Fibre repositories and related communications.

## Reporting

Concerns can be raised with the organisation owners via GitHub.
